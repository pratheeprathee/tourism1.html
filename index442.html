1.Phoenix mall


Phoenix Marketcity is a shopping mall developed by The Phoenix Mills Co.
Ltd., located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
Phoenix Marketcity
Island Star Mall Developers Private Limite
It is the largest mall in Bangalore by area and occupying with total area of 1,000,000 square feet
(93,000 m2) on four floors, which houses 296 stores,
[2] a nine-screen PVR Cinemas multiplex[3] and a food court.

Accidents and incidents

On 28 October 2013, the PVR multiplex along with 54 other shops in the mall were sealed for not
having obtained trade licenses from the Bruhat Bangalore Mahanagara Palike, but later reopened.
[4]On 18 May 2016, Phoenix Mall was raided along with other malls in the city, on a tip-off that
they were ignoring the ban on plastic bags. Phoenix was one of the bigger violators and over 1,000 kg
(2,200 lb) of plastic bags were seized from the mall and a fine imposed.

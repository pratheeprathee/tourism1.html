Matsya Narayana Perumal

1.Matsys nayaranan perual teple

This Temple is located four Kms on the left side after VGP Golden Beach Resort.
The Presiding Deity of this Temple is Matsya Narayana Perumal who is an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.
This Temple is built on a land that was donated to Chinmaya Mission around 25 years ago.
This is a Sea Shore Temple built on the shores of Bay of Bengal. The Idols here are not within the
four walls like in other Temples.
There is also a pebbled path way leading you from one place to the other within the Temple to give
the Devotees an Accupressure Treatment.
The main Idol of Lord Vishnu which is a Matsya or Fish Avatar is carved out of a 12 feet granite
and it is surrounded by 108 other pillars.
Each of the 9 feet pillar has inscriptions drawn from Vishnu Sahasranamam and Ashtothara Sadha
Namavali.
The inscriptions are in Tamil and Sanskrit with an English translation.
Lords Ganesha, Hanuman and Shiva have an Idol in the Meditation Park.
All the Statues are open skywards which means there is no roofing and the entire Temple is open
to the vagaries of nature.
This Temple draws an equal number of Tourists apart from the normal Devotees since it is located
in a Tourist hub on ECR.

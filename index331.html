WONDERLAND PARK

1.WONDERLAND

Wonderla Amusement Park Chennai is the fourth theme park by Wonderla,
after Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Kochi.
But it is more than just an amusement park.
It is the most amazing part of town.
It is a place to celebrate the small joys and share big moments together.
Wonderla is on the outskirts of Chennai, near the Illalur reserve forest.
It offers roller coaster rides, water slides and Ferris wheel, among many
other fun games and activities.
If you don’t want to be a part of the fun, you can simply laze by the pool.
Things to do at Wonderla Amusement Park Chennai Wonderla Amusement Park in
Chennai has the signature land and water rides found in other Wonderla destinations.
From high thrill rides to kid-friendly rides, the theme park has rides to cater
to all age groups.
Also, the different entertainment attractions here are laser light shows,
musical fountains and 4D animation shows, among others.
The amusement park has a Reverse Looping roller coaster ride and Virtual
Reality jungle safari experience too.
Wonderla Theme Park Chennai also has various restaurants serving different
cuisines spread across the park.

Wonderla Amusement Park Chennai Timings


Wonderla in Chennai is open on all days of the week.
On the weekdays, the amusement park is open from 11 AM to 6 PM, and on the
weekends, it is open until 7 PM.
For the best experience, visit Wonderla Theme Park in Chennai in winter
(between November and February).
In the winter months, the weather in Chennai is pleasant. So, you can enjoy
the adventure rides,water slides and the fun games as much as you want.

How to reach Wonderla Amusement Park Chennai?


Wonderla Amusement Park is in Illalur village, on the outskirts of Chennai.
It is 48 km away from Chennai Central railway station, 39 km from Chennai airport
and 56 km from Koyambedu bus stop.
And here are the various ways to reach this theme park –
By bus – Public buses are available to Wonderla Amusement Park Chennai.
Illalur bus stop is only a few hundred metres away from here.
By taxi/cab – The best way to go to Wonderla Amusement Park is by booking a
taxi or full-day cab from top car rental companies in Chennai.
